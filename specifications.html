What's the difference? 😕

To actually learn an instrument, you must first have access to one of them. However, as a beginner, it might be overwhelming with so many options to choose from. Here, you may refer to the table below for a quick overview of the differences between the different types of piano!


Types of Piano Sound Production Average Costs of Owning Number of Keys Regular Tuning Portablility
Acoustic Piano Hammer SGD 5000 to 9000 88 Keys Yes No
Digital Piano Electricity/Pre-recorded SGD 500 to 2000 Usually 88 Keys No Tuning Required Depends
Keyboard Electricity/Pre-recorded SGD 100 to 1000 66/72/88 Keys (Depending on Model) No Tuning Required Yes
