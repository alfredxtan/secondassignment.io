Acoustic Pianos


There generally are two types of acoustic piano: Upright (left) and Grand (right).

The upright piano is the most common one which people usually get. With 88 keys, the upright pianos provide you with a full range of sounds. Also, such pianos produce its sound by utilizing a hammer-action: Hammers within the piano hits the string to produce the beautiful melody you play! However, do note that such pianos are very heavy (weighing over 100kg in some instances!) and require professional movers to bring it from one place to another. Also, they usually carry a relatively high price-tag as well, ranging anywhere from $2000 to $9000!
